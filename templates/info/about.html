Our Story

SoulSight AI was founded in 2023 by a team of AI researchers, designers, and storytellers who shared a common vision: to make advanced AI image understanding accessible to everyone.

We noticed that while AI could recognize objects in images, it often missed the emotional context, the hidden stories, and the deeper meaning that makes images truly powerful. So we set out to create a platform that doesn't just see images, but understands them.

Today, SoulSight AI combines Google's state-of-the-art Gemini Vision technology with our proprietary emotional intelligence algorithms to provide insights that are both technically accurate and emotionally resonant.
